Taniti offers a reliable and affordable transportation system for visitors. Public buses connect **Taniti City, Merriton Landing, and major tourist spots**, while taxis and bike rentals provide flexible options for getting around.

  • Bus Routes: Covering major tourist spots, with express and local options.
  • Fares: One-way ticket: $2, Day pass: $5.
  • Taxi Fares: Starting at $3, with additional charges per mile.
  • Bike Rentals: Daily rental starts at **$10**, including helmet (required by law).
